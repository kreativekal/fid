About

Welcome to the Bestie Book Club! We are a proud group of girls who love to read! Whether you love to read a simple cute rom com or an action packed fantasy book, our book club is the place for you! We have new books every month picked by our members. You can choose to either follow along with our montly picks or read your own books at your own pace. This club has no set rules and is just a fun place to hang out and talk about books.

In our book club we offer:

  • New books every month picked by yup you guessed it... YOU!
  • No set rules or deadlines to finish books
  • A safe and fun place to chat about books
  • Fun events like 24 hour readathons, monthly wrap ups, and more!
